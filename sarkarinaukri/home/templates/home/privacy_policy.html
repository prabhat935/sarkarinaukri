8. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@sarkarinaukri.com

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it promptly.
